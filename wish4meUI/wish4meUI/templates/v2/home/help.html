Wish4me, the best way to share your wishes


Show your friends' wishes

To show your friends' wishes, all you need to do is clicking Friend Activity page. In that page, you can accomplish your friends' wishes. Also you can wish your friends' wishes. It will copy the wish to your default (first) wishlist.


Share your wishes

Wish4me offers you the best way to share your wishes.

In my wishes page, you can create wish via clicking create a wish of yours button.

When creating, you must specify its description, category, wishlist, brand and name. You can also add photos about that wish. You can also set your wish as private if you don't want other people to see your wishes.

Show and edit your wishes

In my wishes page, you can see your wishes. If you wish, you can edit your wishes. In edit page, you can edit your wish's every attribute. You can also edit your wish's photos.

Create wishlists

In manage wishlists page, you can see all of your wishes in corresponding wishlist. You can also edit your wishes here. This page allows you to create wishlists. You can edit them, make them private.

Search wishes, friends

With the textbox at the right top of every page you can search wishes and friends. You can follow them in search page.

Edit your profile

To edit your profile, click your name near search textbox and click profile. You will see your current profile. You can edit your profile via clicking edit profile button.

In edit profile page, you can set your username, password, first name, last name, gender and photo. You can set your photo from gravatar or from your computer.

Also you can activate your other social platform accounts at the right of the edit profile page.

Find your friends

You can find your friends from other social platforms in Wish4meIn in find friends page. In that page, you can select the social platform to import friends and click follow. All you should do is that easy.
